About BLACKPINK

BLACKPINK is a South Korean girl group formed by YG Entertainment, consisting of members Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé, and Lisa. They debuted in August 2016 with their single album "Square One".

Meet the Members

Jisoo

Jisoo is the lead vocalist and visual of BLACKPINK. She was born on January 3, 1995.

Jennie

Jennie is the main rapper and lead vocalist of BLACKPINK. She was born on January 16, 1996.

Rosé

Rosé is the main vocalist and lead dancer of BLACKPINK. She was born on February 11, 1997.

Lisa

Lisa is the main dancer and lead rapper of BLACKPINK. She was born on March 27, 1997.
